Music

Music is an art form and cultural activity that uses organized sound and silence over time. It typically involves elements like rhythm, melody (a sequence of tones), and often harmony (the combination of simultaneous sounds), created using the human voice or a wide variety of musical instruments. Beyond mere sound, music is a universal medium for emotional expression, communication, and entertainment across all human societies.
